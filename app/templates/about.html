About Weather Alert

Weather Alert is a free tool that delivers real-time severe weather warnings from the National Weather Service directly to your inbox.
Our goal is to keep you informed so you can stay safe during changing conditions.

This project was built using Flask, Bootstrap, and Azure with a focus on creating a production-ready, full-stack application that connects to real APIs.
